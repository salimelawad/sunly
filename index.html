About Solar Time

Solar time is based on the position of the sun in the sky. Unlike standard time, which uses fixed time zones, solar time varies based on your exact location on Earth.

Key differences between solar time and standard time:

  • Solar noon (12:00) occurs when the sun is directly overhead
  • Each degree of longitude equals 4 minutes of solar time difference
  • Solar time varies throughout the year due to Earth's elliptical orbit
  • No daylight saving time adjustments needed

This app calculates solar time based on your location's latitude and longitude, taking into account the equation of time to provide accurate solar time readings.
